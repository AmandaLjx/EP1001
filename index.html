Welcome to my website for the module Further Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals :)

Further Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals (EP1001) is the continuation of the elective module Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals (EP1000) which are both offered at Singapore Polytechnic. In this module we will learn things like CNC machining, designing and fabricating custom circuit boards, applications programming and the Internet of Things.
